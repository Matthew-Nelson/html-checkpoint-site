About Me

I'm a San Francisco Bay Area native, who moved with my wife to Templeton in the summer of 2018. I joined the Cal Poly marketing faculty in Spring 2019 after a long professional and teaching career in Silicon Valley.

I love teaching so happy to step in at the eleventh hour with Matt Adams to teach Kinesiology and Public Health majors how to leverage media and technology strategies and tools to support their careers and promote public health-related causes they are passionate about.
